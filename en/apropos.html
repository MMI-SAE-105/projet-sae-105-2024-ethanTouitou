About

*Prehistory of the Future* is an interactive project that explores the evolution of artificial intelligence and its impact on society through the lens of time. It reflects on how our current technologies, especially AI, might be perceived in a distant future, much like how we view prehistoric artifacts today.

Project Concept:

The idea behind *Prehistory of the Future* is to compare artificial intelligence to "fossilized" objects in time, anticipating how future generations might evaluate and interpret our technological advances. This site offers a reflection through various media (objects, films, books, music) on the impact of AI and how it redefines the boundary between humans and machines.

Why Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial intelligence is an essential technology today, integrated into many aspects of our daily lives. It inspires both fascination and concern due to its ability to automate, learn, and mimic human behavior. This project examines its evolution and long-term implications, hoping to better understand how AI might transform the society of the future.
